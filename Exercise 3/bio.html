Biography Page!


This city boy was born and raised in south Detroit. He took a midnight train going anywhere, and this made him into what he is today, a rootin', tootin', six shootin' son-of-a-gun. He is a holy boy, upholding good traditions and sacraments. Still, he keeps the laughs and fun rolling. He has a lot of favorite things, and also loves long walks on the beach.
